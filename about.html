Our Humble Beginnings

From a small boutique in Chennai to becoming a beloved name in Indian fashion

Where Tradition Meets Modernity

Founded in 2010 by Mokshitha Reddy, our journey began with a simple vision: to preserve the rich heritage of Indian textile artistry while making it accessible to the modern woman. What started as a small boutique in Chennai has grown into a trusted name across India, known for exceptional quality and timeless designs.

Each piece in our collection tells a story - of skilled artisans who have preserved traditional techniques for generations, of luxurious fabrics that drape like a dream, and of designs that celebrate the beauty of Indian femininity.

Today, we serve thousands of elegant women who trust us for their most special moments - weddings, festivals, celebrations, and everyday elegance. Our commitment remains unchanged: to bring you clothing that makes you feel confident, beautiful, and truly special.

Meet Our Founder

The visionary behind Mokshitha Collections

Mokshitha Reddy

Founder & Creative Director

"Every woman deserves to feel elegant and confident in her clothing. That belief has been the foundation of everything we create."

With a degree in Textile Design from NIFT and a passion for traditional Indian crafts, Mokshitha started her journey with just five weavers from Kanchipuram. Her dedication to quality, attention to detail, and deep respect for craftsmanship has shaped the brand's identity. Today, she personally oversees every collection, ensuring that each piece meets her exacting standards of excellence.

Our Journey

Milestones in our journey of elegance

2010

The Beginning

Mokshitha Collections opens its first boutique in Chennai with a collection of 50 handpicked sarees. The focus is on authentic Kanchipuram silks and traditional weaves.

2013

First Expansion

We open our second store in Bangalore and launch our first designer collection. Our team grows to include master weavers from Varanasi and artisans from Gujarat.

2016

Digital Transformation

Launch of our e-commerce platform, making Mokshitha Collections accessible nationwide. We introduce "Mokshitha Studio" for contemporary western wear.

2019

Sustainable Initiatives

Introduction of our eco-friendly collection using organic cottons and natural dyes. We partner with artisan cooperatives across 5 states.

2022

International Recognition

Featured in "Vogue India" and "Elle Decor". We begin shipping internationally to the US, UK, and UAE, sharing Indian elegance with the world.

2023

Present Day

Serving over 50,000 customers with 7 physical stores and a thriving online presence. Launch of our bespoke tailoring service and virtual try-on feature.
